Nice to meet you, I'm Sarah.

Life long learner and adventurer, there are few career paths that I have not dabbled in. I entered the work force when I was only 14 working at a family friends pizzaria. From there I moved to working in retail; I did this until I graduated in 2016. After moving and making a few life choices I decided to try out the medical feild as a Certified Nursing assistant. I worked this job for a few years before deciding to attend college for the first time. I attened a community college for a semester or tow part time until deciding to move to Cincinnati where I began working closely in pet care such as dog daycare and boarding as well as professional grooming. I was happy in the animal feild but I craved a more comfortable life and in 2021 I decided to begin attending college again, first at Cincinnati State then at Northern Kentucky University, to aquire a degree in software engineering in hopes of being hired by a tech company and ultimately achieving a higher paying job equating to a more comfortable life.

You can say I am a bit of a nomad. I was born in Atlanta Georgia. I lived there for ten years after which my family and I moved to my parents home town in the beautiful state of Michigan. I spent another ten years there spending a lot of time hiking and exploring the state. Now I live in Cincinnati Ohio in hopes of finding new excitement in this next phase of my life.
